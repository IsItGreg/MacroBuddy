About

In day-to-day work, all software developers will occasionally find themselves repeating the same sequences of terminal commands over and over again. Often our quality of life can be improved by writing a shell script to automate the process; however, the task of writing and debugging shell scripts as well as keeping those scripts organized can sometimes feel as time consuming or more than the task would have taken in the first place. Our team introduces “Macro Buddy,” an application that writes and organizes shell scripts for you!
